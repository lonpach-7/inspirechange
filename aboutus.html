His Transformation Story

Collins did not grow up believing he would one day speak to crowds. His journey began in a quiet room in 2017 with a borrowed set of Global Leadership Summit tapes. As Cory Booker declared, “Now that you are here… what will you do?” — something awakened in him.

That night became the turning point:

  • From insecurity to clarity
  • From confusion to identity
  • From wasted potential to purpose
  • From passive living to intentional growth

Scriptures like Romans 12:2 and Ecclesiastes 9:10 shaped his philosophy: “Excellence is worship. A transformed mind produces a transformed life.”

Raised in a home with emotional gaps despite financial stability, Collins grew up valuing dignity, justice, compassion, and purpose. He promised himself: “If I rise, I will lift others with me.” Today, that promise has become his life’s work.

Turning point — 2017

A borrowed GLS tape & one question changed a life. The rest was discipline, study, prayer, and action.

Official Bio — For Event Organizers

Collins K. Masinde is a mindset transformation speaker who helps youth, faith communities, schools, and corporate teams renew their thinking and live with disciplined excellence. Drawing from Scripture, leadership training, and practical habit formation, Collins combines deep spiritual truth with actionable tools that audiences can apply immediately. His talks are designed to be accessible to believers and non-believers alike — not churchy, not simply motivational, but transformational.

Collins’ ministry emerged after a pivotal moment in 2017. A borrowed set of Global Leadership Summit tapes and one question — “Now that you are here… what will you do?” — set him on a path of intentional growth. From that point he moved from uncertainty to clarity, shaped his values around dignity and service, and committed to lifting others as he rises.

He has since spoken at schools, churches, leadership conferences, and corporate events, focusing on identity, discipline, purpose, and the integration of faith into daily life. Collins is available for keynotes, workshops, leadership training, and virtual sessions. Event organizers appreciate his balance of depth and practicality, and his careful follow-up with audiences and host organizations.
